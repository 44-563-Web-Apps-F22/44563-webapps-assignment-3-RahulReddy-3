Directions from Nearest Airport to the Museum.

The Nearest Airport from the Lowrey Museum is Manchester Airport. The following list gives the step by step directions to reach Lowrey Museum from Manchester Airport.

  1. Head south on Enterprise Wy.
  2. At the roundabout, take the 2nd exit onto the M56 ramp to M60.
  3. Take M56, M60, Exits 7-8 and A56 to The Quays in Salford.
  4. Merge onto Airport Spur/A555.
    1. Keep right at the fork.
    2. follow signs for Manchester/Stockport/M60/Sheffield/M67/M56/Leeds/M62/Bolton/M61.
    3. merge onto M56.
  5. Keep right at the fork to stay on M56.
  6. Continue onto Princess Pkwy/A5103.
  7. Keep right at the fork and merge onto M60.
  8. At the roundabout, take the 1st exit and stay on The Quays.
  9. You will be at the destination.
