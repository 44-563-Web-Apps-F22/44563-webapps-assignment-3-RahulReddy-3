Rocky the Yellowjacket

Rocky the yellowjacket is the Mascot of University of Rochester, Rochester City, NewYork. Rocky is based on the yellowjacket wasp common in North America. Since its creation in the mid-1920s, the mascot has been portrayed in a range of styles, from club-wielding to cuddly. The current version debuted in 2008 to better reflect the fierce competitive spirit of our Yellowjacket athletes.

Given below are the Funfacts about Rocky

  • The previous Rocky outfit had some limitations in terms of being able to do dynamic movements.
  • Rocky used to look less bee-like and less scary before.
  • In 1983, the mascot was name URBee — pronounced “Er-Bee” according to the UR’s University’s “Symbols” page on its website — and was replaced by Rocky the Yellowjacket in 2008.
